Lasanga Recipe

Description

The lasagna has layers of pasta in between layers of beef and sauce. if cooked correctly, the sauce, meat, cheese and pasta make a good combo for a dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 5 pasta sheets
  • A jar of tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • a packet of mozarrella cheese

Steps

  1. cook the pasta and brown the ground beef
  2. place a layer of pasta at the bottom of the pan
  3. AdD a layer of brown ground beef, and cheese and sauce
  4. Repeat the layering process as desired.
